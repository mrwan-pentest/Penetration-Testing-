#  Passive Information Gathering
## Definition

Collecting information without directly interacting with the target.

The target is usually unaware that information is being gathered.

---

## Examples

- Whois lookup
- Google dorking
- LinkedIn searching
- DNS records analysis
- Shodan

---

## Advantage

- Quiet method
- Hard to detect

---

Active Information Gathering

## Definition

Collecting information by directly interacting with the target.

This involves sending requests or packets to the target system.

---

## Examples

- Nmap scanning
- Ping
- Gobuster
- Banner grabbing
- Port scanning

---

## Disadvantage

- Can be detected in logs or by IDS/IPS systems
